View Summary by Teams

This page shows the score obtained by each team that belongs to the same group in a QUESTOURnament. A percentage of this score determined by the "Percentage of Team Scoring" parameter of the QUESTOURnament will contribute to the individual score of each member of the team.

The following parameters are shown:

  • Team: name of the team.
  • Answers: number of answers submitted by the members of the same team in this QUESTOURnament.
  • Assessed Answers: number of answers assessed of all the ones submitted by the members of the team in the QUESTOURnament.
  • Number of Challenges: number of challenges proposed by the members of the team in this QUESTOURnament.
  • Assessed Challenges: number of challenges assessed of all the ones proposed by the members of the team in the QUESTOURnament.
  • Challenges Score total score obtained by the members of the team corresponding to the assessment made by the teachers of the challenges proposed by them.
  • Answers Score: total score obtained by the members of the team corresponding to the assessment of the answers submitted by them.
  • Score: it is the total score, incorporating the challenges and answers scores of each and every member of the team..

By default, the ranking is ordered according to the total score obtained by each team, although this order can be modified by choosing another ordering parameter and pressing its name.

It is also possible to access the individual ranking through the option View Global Classification.
